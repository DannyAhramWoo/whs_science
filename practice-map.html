Two things to know about this list

The mapping was made by reading every question in the app and deciding which chapter it mostly tests. A few questions sit between two chapters, and some sets contain title slides with no question on them at all, so treat the numbers as a good guide rather than an exact index.

Some chapters have no matching questions. That is not a mistake in the list — it means the app simply does not cover that topic yet, which is worth knowing before an exam.
