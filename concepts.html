How to use these pages

The chapters are what you revise from. They are built to be read in order, and they carry the diagrams, the worked examples and the practice. If you want to know what you are expected to understand, go there first, and come back here afterwards.

These pages are for the moment a chapter leaves you wanting more — when an explanation stopped just short of the bit you were curious about, or when something felt true but not quite explained. That is also why each one carries its history. Seeing how an idea was argued over, and what careful people believed before they got it right, makes it far harder to forget than a definition ever could.
